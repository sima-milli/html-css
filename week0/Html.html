Flowers

Holland is famous for its beautiful flower bulb fields that give colour to the typical Dutch landscape. Therefore you can't talk about Holland without talking about the beautiful flowers, The best-known Dutch flower is the tulip. Holland is also famous as the largest flower exporter in the world.
The Westland region and Aalsmeer are the heart of the international flower trade. Here are some of the most popular types :

Tulips

In 1593, the first tulip in Holland could be admired in the Hortus Botanicus in Leiden.
Today, you can enjoy the vast fields of tulip in the BollenstreekAnd at Keukenhof,
where over 7 million flowering bulbs can be admired every year.

Daffodils

the flowers symbolizing friendship,
are some of the most popular flowers exclusively due to their unmatched beauty.
in spring the first daffodils are flowering,they grow in the fields As Tulips.

SunFlower

it's not just a beautiful flower,
it is also the name of two series of still life paintings by the Dutch painter Vincent van Gogh.
